Sweet Carrot Cake

Description

This is an allergy friendly cake and even though it has some unusuall ingrediants it still tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Apple sauce
  • Flour
  • Pinapple puree
  • Fresh carrots

Steps

  1. Chop up carrots into very small pieces
  2. Mix carrots and pinapple in blender with carrots
  3. Pour contents of blender into bowl with flour
  4. mix untill all contents are blended together well
  5. Put in oven for 20 to 30 minutes untill light brown color
  6. Test with toothpick to see if cake is ready

Allergy free Vanilla Frosting

Description

Cakes are always much better with some frosting and fun deigns. You can use this simple and quick allergy friendly recipie to make great frosting for the Sweet Carrot Cake or any other pastry you might want to put frosting on.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 1% milk(Other milks work to but this one usally works the best)
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Leave butter out for 1 hour before cooking to let it defrost
  2. Mix milk butter and brown sugar in bowl for 5 minuites
  3. Once contents in bowl start to mold together use mixmaster. Monitor mix master carfully.
  4. Mix in mix master untill frosting is white and creamy.
  5. If you wish to add colors you can mix food coloring with the frosting
  6. Now put your frosting on your pastry
